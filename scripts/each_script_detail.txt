1. generate_fake_data.py:

Uses Faker to simulate real bank transactions.

Imposes a small percentage of frauds.

Stores the data to CSV.

2. connect_mysql.py:

Uses mysql-connector-python to connect.

Reusable utility for all queries.

3. utils.py

load_csv(): Safely loads any CSV — e.g., raw or processed transactions.

save_csv(): Saves your DataFrame safely — used after cleaning or feature engineering.

print_separator(): Helps keep your Jupyter notebook logs readable.
